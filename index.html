Where can I start learning about stocks?

Ternak Uang

Ternak Uang is a financial education platform to improve the financial literacy of the Indonesian people with a mission to create 10 million investors in Indonesia. Here you can learn investing and personal finance from 0. All in one app with full features to help elevate your investment journey to your version of financial freedom.

RTI Business

This RTI Business application is an app designed to assist stock investors in seeking analysis of the market or stock to be targeted. With this application, data regarding the stocks market will be mostly accurate, including data on fluctuations in the market and other supporting data.

IDX Channel

IDX Channel is a 24-hour business news television station that broadcasts business news and information from the Indonesia Stock Exchange. IDX Channel is the result of collaboration between Media Nusantara Citra and the Indonesia Stock Exchange.

IPOT

IndoPremier is present in the community as a "responsible company" That is a company that is based on one goal to provide benefits and contributions to society ("Purpose-Driven-Company"), not just a company that is only profit-oriented ("traditional-for-profit-driven-company"). IndoPremier's Purpose is reflected in its principle, namely "To Empower society to take control of their financial lives."
